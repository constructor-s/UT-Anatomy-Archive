Question 1

1. From the following options, choose all statements that correctly describe dendrites.

Choices:

  • are highly branched
  • the amplitude of electrical signals in this region codes intensity
  • usually one per cell
  • can be very long and therefore transmit information between different regions of the body
  • may be myelinated or unmyelinated

Answers:

  • are highly branched
  • the amplitude of electrical signals in this region codes intensity

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! Dendrites are numerous and highly branched. They conduct graded electrical signals that are proportional to the strength of stimulus.
