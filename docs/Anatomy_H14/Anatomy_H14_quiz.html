Question 1

Which of the following regions of the digestive tract include an adventitia?

Choices:

  • the esophagus
  • the sigmoid colon
  • the ileum
  • the stomach

Answers:

  • the esophagus

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! Portions of the digestive tract that are intraperitoneal include a serosa, but those that are not, like the esophagus, have an adventitia.
