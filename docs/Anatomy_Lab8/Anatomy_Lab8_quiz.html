Question 5

5. The second heart sound, “dub”, occurs at the beginning of ventricular ____________ (systole or diastole).

Choices:

  • diastole

Answers:

  • diastole

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! At the beginning of diastole, the reversed pressure differential between the ventricle and efferent vessels results in blood filling the sinuses of the semilunar valves. Closure of the valves results in the ‘dub’ sound.
