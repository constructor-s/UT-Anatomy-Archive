Question 1

1. What does the perisinusoidal space of Disse contain?

Choices:

  • plasma
  • lymph
  • bile
  • blood

Answers:

  • plasma

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! The space of Disse is the extracellular space between the hepatocytes and the sinusoidal endothelium. Plasma extravasates through the leaky walls of the sinusoids.
