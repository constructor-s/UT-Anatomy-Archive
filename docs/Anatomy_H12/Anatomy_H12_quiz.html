Question 1

Choose one INCORRECT statement regarding the uterine cycle.

Choices:

  • The secretory phase of the uterine cycle is stimulated primarily by rising estrogen levels.
  • During the proliferative phase, the functional zone of the endometrium regenerates from the basal zone.
  • The proliferative phase of the uterine cycle coincides with the follicular phase of the ovarian cycle.
  • The bases of the uterine glands remain intact following menstruation.

Answers:

  • The secretory phase of the uterine cycle is stimulated primarily by rising estrogen levels.

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! The secretory phase of the uterine cycle is stimulated by progesterones secreted by the corpus luteum.
