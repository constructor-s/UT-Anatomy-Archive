19A Quiz

Question 1

1. In a lower motor neuron lesion, reflexes are ______________.

Choices:

  • decreased
  • normal
  • increased

Answers:

  • decreased

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! The deep tendon reflex arc requires an intact lower motor neuron (LMN), therefore a lesion of the LMN will decrease or abolish reflexes.
  • Continue

Choices:

  • decreased
  • normal
  • increased

Answers:

  • increased

Comments:

  • Correct
  • That's right! Reflexes are normally suppressed by input from UMNs, therefore lesion will increase reflexes.
  • Continue

Question 3

Choices:

  • decreased
  • normal
  • increased

  • normal

Comments:
